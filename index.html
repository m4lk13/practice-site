Services

How we support market access.

A focused set of services for entering and operating in the Russian and EAEU life-sciences market. Exact scope and deliverables are agreed with each client.

01

Regulatory & market-access advisory — pharma

RU / EAEU registration pathways, reimbursement listing (ЖНВЛП) and localisation requirements for pharmaceutical products.

02

Medical-device registration & compliance

Roszdravnadzor and EAEU device pathways — technical files, conformity, and post-market vigilance documentation.

03

Pharmacoeconomics & HTA support

Cost-effectiveness and budget-impact analysis, value dossiers and evidence packages for reimbursement and formulary decisions.

04

Regulatory documentation & translation

Dossier support (including eCTD-aligned structure) and technical / medical RU ↔ EN translation and document preparation.

05

Russian market support

Supplier and CMO identification, contract-manufacturing localisation, and documentation updates to current RU / EAEU requirements.

06

Market research & financial modelling

Market sizing, forecasting, pricing scenarios and decision-ready business cases — segment analysis of the pharma market.

07

Public affairs & stakeholder engagement

Compliant liaison with industry associations and regulatory bodies; international research channels (incl. Singapore) on request.

Distinctive competence

Digital analytics at the IT × healthcare intersection

Interactive dashboards and automated pipelines for collecting and processing data — tenders / ЕИС, customs statistics, open registries — plus predictive models of access and demand. International research channels (incl. Singapore / ASEAN) engaged where needed.
